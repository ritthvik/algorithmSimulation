KRUSKAL'S VISUALISATION

Steps to use the simulator.

  1. Enter the number of nodes in the graph.
  2. Press "Add Edges" to continue.
  3. Enter the edge between the nodes and press "Save Edges".
  4. Press "Start" to start the simulation.
  5. Keep pressing the "simulate" button for the simulation.
